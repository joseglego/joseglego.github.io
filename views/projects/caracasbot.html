About the Project

It was my first national web system, actually we receive persons of Colombia in this venezuelan robotics competition. You can registered on the system and can invite persons to your team or manage the team documents and your presonal documents, requirments of competition as payment bouchers, documentations. And it generate the list of persons whom go to the competition, access area list. I was code the enterely system with Django & jQuery, plus I made the deployment and sys admin of the server.
