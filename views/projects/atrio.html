About the Project

Atrio Seguros is a growing insurance company in Venezuela. In his second year they included this web system. The idea is their agents can access through internet (Portable Devices & Computers) and can make budgets and generate insurance policies for Cars. We advanced the Insurance policies for people & claim reports, both funcionalities are in Beta Testing.

The main idea of the project is connect to Acsel X (a legacy Insurance Software) and can make an improve application whom can connect and interact with Acsel through an RESTful API based on Java.

It was my first commercial project.

What I did
  • Front-End Developer, with AngularJS. Implementing a lot of funcionalities (only 2 developers)
  • Create & Fix Bugs in the PL/SQL of the RESTful API.
