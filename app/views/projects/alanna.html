About the Project

Renamed as Travel Contact. Alanna is a tourism startup which can help you manage your travel agency, your clients and their trips. The idea is, you can access the whole information of your agency anytime and anywhere (you need internet connection). You can check the information of your clients, previous trips, interests and what they really like and with this you can promote new trips. Also, you can manage the costs of each trip, the profits of the agency and the profit of the advisor. All, in just one system.

  • Scrum Master, in one of the 2 developments teams, teaching my team and reviewing its code. Also, including tools.
  • Improving the auth & session using Devise and CanCanCan

  • Creating small applications from scratch to help our core application.
