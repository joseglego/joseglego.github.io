About the Project

Alanna is a tourism startup based on the issue the tourism agent lost all the notes about previous projects. The idea, is you can access all the information of your agency wherever you are and whenever you need. You can check the information of your clients, previous travels, interests and what they really like.

Plus, you can create new travels, with a lot of services and the special information, can share the information travel and itinerary of the clients withou left the platform.

Also, your clients can access and help you to set the information (like Visas & Passports.Inclusive from their portable devices in Beta Testing.
